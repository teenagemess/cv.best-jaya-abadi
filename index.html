ABOUT US

Know About Our History

Established on May 31, 2022, as a national company engaged in agrichemicals, agriculture, livestock, agribusiness, equipment supply, commodities, and training.

We have established partnerships with various companies, farmer groups, and livestock farmers in Indonesia. Through these business activities, we are committed to making a tangible contribution to improving the national economy, particularly in the agriculture and livestock sectors.
